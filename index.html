Welcome to Badian National High School

Announcements

Badian National High School(BNHS) is a public secondary school located in Poblacion, Badian, Cebu, Philippines. The school has a rich history and has been an important institution in the community for many years.

The exact establishment date of BNHS is unclear, but it is known to have been founded in the early 1900s. Initially, it started as an elementary school, and eventually expanded to include secondary education. Over the years, the school has gone through various developments and improvements to provide better education for the students.

Throughout its history, BNHS has produced many successful alumni who have gone on to contribute to various fields and industries. The school has also been recognized for its academic excellence, as well as its promotion of the arts, sports, and culture.

In terms of infrastructure, the school has undergone several renovations and expansions to accommodate the increasing number of students. It has well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, computer laboratories, and other facilities necessary for effective education.

Today, Badian National High School continues to provide quality education and contributes to the overall growth and development of the students in Badian, Cebu. It remains a pillar of the community, shaping the future of its students and making a positive impact in society.
